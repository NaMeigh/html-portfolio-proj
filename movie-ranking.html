My Top 3 movies of all time

Hachiko

This is a story of an Akita dog named Hachiko who was very loyal to his master who suddenly died. Hachicko waited for his master's return everyday at the same train station for almost 10 years, until he died.

Turbo

This is an animated 2013 film about a snail who never gives up on his dream and believed that he can be a race champion.

Dog Gone

This is a 2022 Netflix Film about a dog who got lost and the unwavering search that his owners did to find him back.
